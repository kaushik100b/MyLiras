About MyLiras

MyLiras is a digital marketplace where everyday social media users can earn by sharing brand content, while businesses get affordable, authentic promotion from real people. It’s a platform built on trust, transparency, and results.

Who We Are

MyLiras is a digital platform that connects social media users (influencers) with businesses looking to promote their services or products in an affordable and impactful way. We offer a bridge between everyday Instagram users and companies, creating a win-win situation for both parties.

Monetization for users through paid Instagram stories or posts.

Low-cost marketing for companies via user-generated content.

A secure, fraud-protected system to ensure authenticity.

Live dashboards to track campaigns, earnings, and engagement.

How It Works

Users sign up, connect their Instagram account, and start earning by uploading
content provided by businesses. Businesses register to upload templates and
manage their campaigns from their dashboard.

Security and
Fairness

We have built-in tools to detect fake followers and fake engagement. Users attempting to manipulate their metrics will be automatically banned, and any earnings forfeited. This guarantees that only real, valuable promotion occurs.

How Users
Earn

Earnings are calculated based on two main factors: the number of followers and the engagement rate. Users receive money in their internal wallet and can withdraw or redeem it for gift cards.

Campaigns for
Businesses

Companies can create campaigns by uploading visual templates and choosing their budget and target audience. Each campaign is published and promoted through the users' Instagram accounts.
